A near-complete specimen of Tyrannosaurus rex, nicknamed "Stan" has been sold for a world record price of $31.8m (24.6m euros).





NAME: Tyrannosaurus (tie-RAN-oh-sore-us)

NAME MEANING: ('tyrant lizard')

LENGTH: Up to 12.0m

WEIGHT: 7000kg

TYPE OF DINOSAUR: large theropod

KINGDOM(CLASSIFICATION): Animalia

VEGITATION: CARNIVORE

EATS: Triceratops,velociraptor

HABITAT:(PLACE OF RESIDING) Canada, USA

AVERAGE LIFESPAN: about 28-30 years.

TIME OF EXTINSION: 65 million years ago

REASON OF EXTINSION: went extinct during the K-T mass extinction, about 65 million years ago

ENEMIES: Giganotosaurus
